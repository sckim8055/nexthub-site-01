Our Services

End-to-end IT services built for multinational companies operating in Korea

IT Onsite & Remote Support

We put a dedicated IT engineer in your Korea office — someone who speaks your language and follows your HQ's standards.

  • L1/L2 helpdesk — PC, network, printer, software issues
  • Ticket management on ServiceNow or your existing ITSM tool
  • Flexible engagement: full-time, scheduled visits, or remote
  • Wifi survey, server room check, IT equipment dispatch
  • New hire IT setup & offboarding asset collection

Cybersecurity & Compliance

We make sure your Korea office meets HQ security policies and local regulations — without your HQ needing to micromanage it.

  • Endpoint protection rollout and daily monitoring
  • Network vulnerability scanning & remediation
  • HQ security policy localization & enforcement
  • Security incident handling & root cause reporting
  • Staff security training (phishing, data handling, etc.)

IT Consulting & Project Management

Need to launch a new system, migrate platforms, or align Korea IT with global strategy? We manage it end to end.

  • IT roadmap planning aligned with HQ strategy
  • System integration (SI) project execution
  • Requirements gathering & business process analysis
  • PMO operation, budget planning (CAPEX/OPEX)
  • Local vendor sourcing, evaluation & management

IT Outsourcing & SPOC

Don't need a full IT department? We act as your Korea IT team — handling everything from daily ops to HQ reporting under one contract.

  • Full or partial IT operations takeover
  • SPOC: one contact for everything IT in Korea
  • Infra, cloud & network managed services
  • Monthly operations report & SLA dashboard
  • Trilingual HQ communication (KR / EN / CN)
